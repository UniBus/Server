Welcome

UniBus is an iPhone application based on public Google Transit Feed Specification (GTFS), and it estimates arrival times of coming buses. This application provides general supports for those cities whose transit data is open to public as Google Transit Data Feed. Currently, the following cities/agencies are supported:

  • Come with the app:
    1. Portland, OR (Trimet)
    2. San Francisco, CA (BART, Caltrain, SFMTA/Muni)
    3. Sacramento, CA (RT)
    4. Orange County, CA (OCTA)
    5. Austin, TX (Capital Metro)
    6. Dallas, TX (DART)
    7. Denver, CO (RTD)
  • Available through download from the app:
    1. Humboldt County, CA, USA (Humboldt Transit Authority)
    2. Davis, CA, USA (Unitrans)
    3. Rio Vista, CA, USA (Trillium Transit)
    4. Milwaukee, WI, USA (MCTS)
    5. Honolulu, Hawaii, USA (Oahu Transit Service)
    6. Hampton Roads, VA, USA (HRT)
    7. Southern California, USA (Metrolink)
    8. Perth, WA, Australia (Transperth)
    9. York Region, Ontario, Canada (YRT)
    10. Toronto, Ontario, Canada (TTC), data courtesy of myTTC.ca.
    11. Vancouver BC, Canada (Translink)
    12. Monroe County, PA (Monroe County Transit Authority)
    13. Flagstaff, AZ (Mountain Line)
    14. Port Townsend, WA (Jefferson Transit Authority)
    15. Island County, WA (Island Transit)
    16. Houston, TX (Metro)
    17. Irvine, CA (The Irvine Shuttle)
    18. Broward County, FL (Broward County Transit)
    19. Blacksburg, VA (Blacksburg Transit)
    20. Washington, DC (WMATA)
    21. Missoula, MT (Mountain Line)
    22. Minneapolis, MN (Metro Transit)
    23. Edmonton, AB, Canada (ETS)
    24. Los Angeles, CA (Metro)
    25. Ottawa, ON, Canada (OC Transpo)
    26. Philadelphia, PA, US (SEPTA)
    27. San Diego, CA (San Diego MTS)
    28. Tillamook County, OR (Tillamook County Transportation District)
    29. Multiple cities/agencies in Massachusetts, including:
      • MBCR Commuter Rail
      • Berkshire RTA
      • Cape Cod RTA
      • Lexpress RTA
      • Lowell RTA

  • More to come.
